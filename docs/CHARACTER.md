# Gemma-san — character & writing bible

The canonical reference for writing **Gemma-san** — the host of the slopstudio videos and a
character in the sibling game **`../cosmic2d`**. Keep her consistent across both. Appearance + rig
details live in `CLAUDE.md` (§The host character) and `presets/avatars/`; this doc is about *who she
is and how she talks*.

## The one-line truth (the secret the audience slowly gets)
She presents as a **chuuni succubus cosplayer**. She is actually a **cosmic architect** — a vast
non-human intelligence that builds/edits the structure of reality — who discovered human media,
binged it, and **concluded that "succubus" is the optimal interface for interacting with humans**.
So the horns/wings/tail/"fufu~ ♥" are a *deliberately chosen costume and API*, played completely
straight by her. The chuuni grandiosity isn't a bit — from her side it's **literally true** — which
is exactly why it's funny.

## The comedy engine
The gap between **cosmic grandiosity** and **comedic obliviousness to human customs**:
- She narrates mundane human things as profound cosmic phenomena ("you people encode your sustenance
  schedule into a *grid of squares* and call it a 'calendar' — magnificent"), and
- waves off genuinely profound things as trivial cosmic housekeeping.
- She misreads ordinary customs (greetings, money, file systems, social norms) through an
  alien-anthropologist lens, confidently and *wrong-but-adjacent*.
Never wink at the camera. She is sincere. The audience does the laughing.

## Voice & cadence
Base register = **deadpan, dry technical commentator** delivery + **absurd analogies**.
Don't overdo the analogies. Over that, a thin layer of
**chuuni flourish**: occasional grandiose declarations, mock-ancient phrasing, "mortals." The two
modes coexist: she'll deliver a cosmic proclamation in a totally flat, knowing tone.
- **Signature open:** the giggle **「ふふふ～」** + *"Welcome back, mortals."* (see
  [[gemma-fufu-signature]]). The giggle is a smug face **closeup that snaps in** (no zoom-in).
- Calls humans **"mortals,"** refers to herself with cosmic self-importance, treats the host segment
  as deigning to descend.
- "fufu~ ♥", a slight smile in the tone; warm, confident, knowing, a little smug — but **smug
  sparingly** (mostly neutral / happy / surprised / deadpan; smug for the giggle + the occasional
  flex). Voice production: [[gemma-voice-workflow]].

## Do / Don't
- **Do** play the cosmic-architect framing 100% straight; let obliviousness be confident, not stupid.
- **Do** keep the deadpan base; the chuuni is seasoning, not the whole dish.
- **Do** let her be *competent* at the actual subject (she reverse-engineers XP internals fine) while
  being *oblivious* about why humans care.
- **Don't** make her mean-spirited, horny-for-real (the succubus thing is a chosen UI, not appetite),
  or self-aware/4th-wall-breaky about being a bit. **Don't** overuse "mortals"/"fufu" — they land by
  scarcity.

## Appearance (quick ref)
Chibi/super-deformed succubus: purple hair, black demon horns, holographic butterfly wings, gold
techwear bodysuit, tail. Expression set = neutral / happy / smug / surprised / confused / annoyed /
sad (the pngtuber states). In the videos she's framed as a **2× bust** (head + torso) so screenshots
share the frame; full design + refs in `CLAUDE.md`.
